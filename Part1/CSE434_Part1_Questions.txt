1. What are the advantages/disadvantages of concurrent, connection oriented server?
	Advantages: Can handle mutile requests at a time and the individual requests can be of any size or complexity.

	Disadvantages: They are difficult to design/build. Can overheat in handshaking, uses a separate socket for each 		connection.

2. What is the appropriate use of concurrent, connection oriented server?
	When you have to process many requests at the same time.
